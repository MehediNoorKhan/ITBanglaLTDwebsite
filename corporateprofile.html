Corporate Profile

PROFILE

IT Bangla Ltd, a leading IT education, international testing, and software solutions provider in Dhaka, Bangladesh, has over 20 years of experience. Since its founding, the company has grown significantly, with an annual enrollment increase of twelve percent. A skilled management team and a qualified Board of Directors oversee operations. Turnkey projects are managed by a dedicated project management team, including a Project Director, Technical Director, Project Manager, and Coordinator, supported by proficient IT, administration, finance, research, and marketing staff.

BACKGROUND

Established in 2000, the esteemed institution located in Dhaka, Bangladesh, serves as a leading authority in IT education, software solutions, and comprehensive IT services. With a clear vision of nurturing a proficient IT workforce to propel the nation's IT industry forward, the organization, since its inception, has remained committed to delivering exceptional services. Over the years, it has successfully trained and developed tens of thousands of skilled IT professionals, who now play integral roles in the ICT industry, both domestically and internationally.
