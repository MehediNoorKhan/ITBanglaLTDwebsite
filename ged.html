GED

In Bangladesh, the General Educational Development (GED) test offers an alternative to the traditional high school diploma, providing a second chance for individuals who did not complete their secondary education. Administered by the American Council on Education (ACE) through authorized centers, the GED is recognized as an equivalent to the high school diploma, opening doors to higher education and better employment opportunities. The exam assesses proficiency in four subject areas: Mathematical Reasoning, Reasoning Through Language Arts, Science, and Social Studies. Successful completion of the GED can significantly enhance the educational and professional prospects of individuals in Bangladesh.

Elements

Mathematical Reasoning: Quantitative problem-solving, algebraic expressions, functions, and geometry

Reasoning Through Language Arts: Reading comprehension, writing, grammar, and editing

Science: Life science, physical science, earth and space science

Social Studies: U.S. history, civics and government, economics, geography, and world history

Benefits

Educational Advancement: Earning a GED credential allows individuals to pursue higher education opportunities.

Career Opportunities: A GED meets basic educational requirements for many jobs, enhancing employment prospects

Higher Earning Potential: Individuals with a GED typically have higher earning potential than those without a high school diploma

Personal Fulfillment: Completing the GED provides a sense of accomplishment and boosts confidence

Flexibility: The GED program offers flexible preparation and testing options to suit individual schedules

IT Bangla LTD. is very pleased to offer GED preparation courses, providing experienced instructors, comprehensive study materials, and flexible schedules. Their interactive learning environment and support services, including counseling and career guidance, ensure students are well-prepared to earn their GED credentials and advance their educational and career goals.
