GIAC

GIAC (Global Information Assurance Certification) is a leading provider of cybersecurity certifications and training programs. Founded by the SANS Institute, GIAC offers over 30 specialized certifications covering various aspects of information security, including incident response, forensics, penetration testing, security administration, and more. GIAC certifications are highly respected in the cybersecurity industry, validating the skills and expertise of professionals worldwide and helping organizations identify qualified cybersecurity talent.

We provide three types of Certifications. Those are:

Practitioner Certification

Applied Knowledge Certification

Portfolio Certification

Offered Certificates

Offensive operations

IT Bangla LTD. delivers training for the GIAC Offensive Operations Certification, equipping individuals with advanced knowledge in offensive cybersecurity tactics. Acquire proficiency in penetration testing, exploit creation, and sophisticated attack strategies.

Cyber defense

IT Bangla LTD. delivers GIAC Cyber Defense certification courses, equipping individuals with advanced proficiencies in cybersecurity defense methodologies, incident response protocols, threat identification, and the administration of network security. Enhance your expertise in cybersecurity defense strategies through our comprehensive training programs.

Cloud Security

At IT Bangla LTD., we specialize in delivering GIAC Cloud Security Certification training, equipping individuals with the expertise needed to safeguard cloud infrastructures effectively. Elevate your professional profile and advance your career by mastering the intricacies of cloud security through our comprehensive program.

Industry control systems

We offer specialized training for GIAC Industrial Control Systems (ICS) Certification, focusing on cybersecurity for critical infrastructure environments, including SCADA systems, industrial protocols, and risk management in ICS.

Digital forensics and incident response

IT Bangla LTD. delivers tailored courses focused on obtaining the prestigious GIAC Digital Forensics and Incident Response (DFIR) certification. Dive deep into cyber incident investigations, evidence handling, and rapid response strategies to combat security breaches effectively and protect digital assets.

IT Bangla LTD. proudly presents a range of coveted certifications, empowering individuals with specialized knowledge and skills. Elevate your career with our comprehensive certification programs tailored to meet industry demands.
