Pearson VUE

Pearson VUE

Pearson VUE stands as a global leader in computer-based testing and assessment services, delivering secure and reliable exam solutions worldwide. With a vast network spanning over 180 countries, it offers a wide array of exams, ranging from professional certifications to academic admissions and government testing. Leveraging advanced technology and secure testing platforms, Pearson VUE ensures a smooth and efficient testing experience for candidates, alongside convenient scheduling options and stringent exam security measures. Serving as a trusted partner to certification bodies, educational institutions, and government agencies, Pearson VUE plays a pivotal role in aiding individuals in achieving their professional and educational aspirations.

Certifications

Professional Certifications: Certificates of different aspects etc.

Academic Admissions: GRE, TOEFL, PTE tec.

Licensure and Certification: Accounting, Nursing, Teaching etc.

Information Technology (IT): Cisco, Microsoft, Oracle, RedHat, Google Cloud etc.

Healthcare: American Red Cross Certifications, DHA (Dubai Health Authority) Examinations, PSI (Psychological Services Incorporated) Examinations etc.

Finance and Accounting: Certified Public Accountant (CPA), Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA), Certified Internal Auditor (CIA) etc.

Project Management: Certified Associate in Project Management (CAPM), Agile and Scrum Certifications, Certified Project Manager (CPM) etc.

Government Testing: Civil Service Exams, Law Enforcement and Public Safety, Foreign Service Officer Test (FSOT) etc.

Language Proficiency: IELTS, PTE (Academic), PTE(General)

Assessment Services: GED Testing Service, HiSET Exam, U.S. Department of State Foreign Service Officer Test (FSOT) etc.
