CISCO

cisco

Cisco Systems, Inc. is a global leader in networking technology, specializing in designing, manufacturing, and selling networking hardware, software, telecommunications equipment, and other high-technology services and products. Founded in 1984 by Leonard Bosack and Sandy Lerner, Cisco pioneered the concept of a local area network (LAN) being used to connect geographically disparate computers over a multiprotocol router system. Headquartered in San Jose, California, the company has grown to become a dominant player in the industry, offering solutions that enhance internet connectivity, cybersecurity, collaboration, and data center management. Cisco's innovations have been integral in advancing internet and communication technologies worldwide.

Offered Certificates

Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA)

The Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA) certification validates foundational networking skills, covering network fundamentals, IP connectivity, security, automation, and programmability, making it essential for entry-level network professionals.

Cisco Certified Network Professional (CCNP)

The Cisco Certified Network Professional (CCNP) certification validates advanced networking skills. It offers various specializations like Enterprise, Security, and Data Center, enhancing career opportunities for experienced networking professionals.

Cisco Certified Internetwork Expert (CCIE)

The Cisco Certified Internetwork Expert (CCIE) is a prestigious certification, demonstrating expert-level knowledge and skills in complex networking, making it one of the most respected credentials in the IT industry.

Cisco Certified Specialist

The Cisco Certified Specialist certification validates expertise in specific technical areas, offering specializations in domains like Enterprise Core, Security Core, Data Center Core, and Collaboration Core for targeted professional growth.

Cisco Certified DevNet Associate and Professional

Cisco Certified DevNet Associate and Professional certifications focus on network automation and programmability, covering software development, APIs, automation, and Cisco platforms, essential for modern network and IT professionals.

Cisco Certified CyberOps Associate

The Cisco Certified CyberOps Associate certification focuses on cybersecurity operations, covering areas such as security monitoring, threat analysis, and incident response, preparing professionals for roles in cybersecurity defense and operations.

These certifications enhance skills in networking, cybersecurity, and automation, equipping professionals with industry-recognized credentials essential for advancing their careers in IT and networking domains.
